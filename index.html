About Nivya Hospitals

About Nivya Respiratory
& Multispeciality Hospital

Nivya Respiratory Centre was established in 1994 with a vision to provide specialized pulmonary care in Tirunelveli. Founded by Dr. P. Manimozhian (MD Chest Medicine), the hospital has grown into a trusted multispeciality healthcare center.

Our Mission

To deliver compassionate respiratory and multispeciality healthcare through advanced diagnostics, experienced doctors, and modern medical technology, ensuring every patient receives accurate treatment & quality care.

Our Vision

To become a trusted center for respiratory and multispeciality healthcare by combining expert medical knowledge, innovative technology, and patient-focused services that improve health and quality of life.
