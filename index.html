About Nivya Hospitals

Excellence in
Respiratory & Multispeciality
Healthcare in Tirunelveli

A symbol of trust and care in Tirunelveli, Nivya Respiratory and Multispeciality Hospitals has built a strong reputation for delivering advanced medical services with a compassionate approach. Since 1994, we have remained committed to providing reliable, ethical and patient focused healthcare.

What began as a dedicated respiratory care center has grown into a well established multispeciality hospital that addresses a wide range of medical needs. Over the years, we have introduced advanced diagnostic and treatment services to the region, strengthening our expertise in pulmonary care.

Our strength lies in our team of experienced doctors, skilled medical professionals and dedicated support staff who work together to ensure every patient receives timely and effective care. By combining modern medical technology with a personalized approach, we make treatment both efficient and comfortable.

With well equipped facilities, continuous emergency support, and a strong focus on affordability, we strive to make quality healthcare accessible to all. Every service we provide is designed with a clear purpose to improve the health and well being of our patients.

At Nivya Hospitals, we believe healthcare goes beyond treatment. It is about building trust, showing compassion, and delivering consistent care. This commitment has made us a dependable healthcare choice for families across Tirunelveli.
