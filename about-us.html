Welcome to

Nivya Hospitals

Nivya Respiratory and Multispeciality Hospitals stands as a trusted name in advanced healthcare in Tirunelveli. Established with a vision to deliver specialised respiratory care, we have grown over the years into a well respected multispeciality hospital known for our commitment to quality treatment and patient well being. With decades of experience in pulmonary care, Nivya Hospitals has played a key role in introducing advanced diagnostic and treatment services in the region. Our focus on respiratory medicine, combined with multispeciality expertise, allows us to provide comprehensive healthcare solutions under one roof.

Our hospital is equipped with modern medical infrastructure, including advanced pulmonary function testing, sleep study facilities, and specialised diagnostic services for accurate and timely treatment. Supported by a dedicated emergency care system and essential critical care facilities, we ensure that patients receive immediate and effective medical attention whenever needed. Backed by a team of experienced doctors, skilled healthcare professionals and supportive staff, we strive to deliver care that is both efficient and compassionate. Every aspect of our service is designed to make the patient experience comfortable, reliable, and outcome focused.

At Nivya Hospitals, we combine medical expertise with a human touch. Our goal is to make advanced healthcare accessible and affordable while maintaining the highest standards of care. Experience healthcare built on trust, driven by innovation, and delivered with compassion at Nivya Respiratory and Multispeciality Hospitals, a dependable healthcare partner for the people of Tirunelveli.

Our Vision

Nivya Respiratory and Multispeciality Hospital will be the premier leader in providing excellent multispeciality care for all those who seek it.

To deliver ethical and high-quality medical care to patients and their families, providing close to home services with compassion and concern, at a reasonable cost.

Year-by-Year Milestones

Each year stands for a new first in our journey of pulmonary innovation.

1994

Exclusive pulmonology concept in tirunelveli.

1997

1st computerized pulmonary function test (κιτ μ - italy) in tirunelveli.

2002

1st allergy physician (trained in nrtc, warwick, uk) in tirunelveli.

2003

1st bronchoscopist (pentax, japan) in tirunelveli

2006

1st sleep study centre (emblata somnologica) in tirunelveli. & 1st exclusive pulmonary inpatient hospital in tirunelveli.

2008

1st pulmonary hospital to have all rooms oxygen service.

2013

1st private hospital to have tb pcr (genexpert) in tirunelveli.

2020–2022

1st private hospital that never shut the doors during covid-19 pandemic.

2023

1st hospital to have feno, ios and 6mwt (types of pulmonary function tests) in tirunelveli.

2024

1st hospital to have latent tb infection blood test (igra).

2025

1st hospital to have fully air-conditioned reception in tirunelveli.
